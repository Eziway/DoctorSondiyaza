Who we are

Dr Sondiyaza Surgery is a modern medical practice dedicated to providing comprehensive, patient focused healthcare to individuals and families. With two conveniently located practices in Tokoza and Alberton, we are committed to delivering accessible, high quality medical services that address both immediate health concerns and long term wellness. Our approach combines professional medical expertise, compassionate care, and a strong focus on preventative health to ensure every patient receives the best possible treatment.
