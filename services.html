Our Services

Comprehensive occupational health solutions designed to ensure workplace safety, compliance, and employee well being.

Pre-Employment Medical Examinations

Medical assessments to determine if candidates are fit for specific job roles before hiring.

Periodic Medical Surveillance

Regular health evaluations to monitor employee fitness and detect early signs of occupational illness.

Exit Medical Examinations

Final assessments conducted when employees leave the company to evaluate work related health impacts.

Fitness for Duty Assessments

Determining whether employees are medically capable of performing specific job functions safely.

Occupational Health Risk Assessments

Identifying workplace hazards and evaluating their impact on employee health and safety.

Biological Monitoring

Testing for exposure to harmful substances such as chemicals, toxins, or hazardous materials.

Exposure Monitoring

Tracking and assessing employee exposure levels to occupational health risks in the workplace.

Occupational Health Reporting

Detailed medical reports to support compliance, audits, and informed decision making.

Employee Vaccination Programmes

Comprehensive vaccination services for employees to ensure workplace health and compliance with occupational health requirements.
