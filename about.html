What We Do

Comprehensive Occupational Medical Practitioner services focused on workplace health, safety, and compliance.

We provide comprehensive Occupational Medical Practitioner services focused on safeguarding the health, safety, and well being of employees across various industries. Our work ensures that individuals are medically fit for their roles while helping organisations meet legal and workplace health requirements.

Our services include pre employment, periodic, and exit medical examinations, as well as fitness for duty assessments tailored to specific job demands. We also conduct occupational health risk assessments to identify workplace hazards and recommend appropriate medical surveillance programs.

In addition, we offer biological monitoring, exposure tracking, and detailed reporting to support informed decision making by employers. Every service is delivered with accuracy, confidentiality, and a strong commitment to occupational health standards, ensuring both compliance and long-term workforce well-being.
